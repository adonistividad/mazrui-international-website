Charbel E. Khoury
Group CEO

Charbel El Khoury is Group CEO of Mazrui International, a role that enables him to use his 19 years of business and law experience to make a compelling contribution to the UAE economy. Based in Abu Dhabi, Mazrui International is a diverse family owned Group, with significant businesses in the energy, industrial, real estate and trading sectors. Additionally, the Group manages a wide range of investments in well-known enterprises across the UAE economy. Charbel guides the Group’s overall direction, growth and priorities, taking the leading role in its strategy, operations, mergers and acquisitions, project finance and joint ventures. Charbel gained his law experience in prominent legal practices in Lebanon and the UAE before assuming the role of Chief Legal Officer at Mazrui International. With commercial responsibilities crossing multiple jurisdictions and industry sectors, this experience helped hone Charbel’s razor-sharp business acumen, which enables him to win as Group CEO today. In addition to his responsibilities as Group CEO, Charbel holds a number of board positions across international organizations including, Hilti Emirates, Air Liquide, Action International, Carbon Holdings, Gulf Refining Company NV, ISO Octane and Union Iron & Steel. Charbel has a Bachelor’s degree in International Law and Legal Studies, and a Master’s degree in Private Law, both from Sagesse University.

WHO WE ARE

Mazrui International is a privately-held diversified company that operates across numerous industries and asset classes. It has grown both organically and inorganically into a significant contributor to, and key player in, the UAE economy. By building long-term relationships with local and international partners, we grow brands and companies and uncover new business opportunities. Our companies are market leaders in several different sectors, including, but not limited to Oil & Gas, Construction & Building Materials, Retail, Industrial and Healthcare. All companies benefit from a world class support structure delivered through a "Shared Services" model. Our industrial expertise and knowledge of local markets, combined with a strong investment reputation and a committed team of professionals makes us ideal partners to invest with in the region.



WHAT WE DO

Mazrui International will create value for its shareholders and distinguish itself as one of the premier diversified companies in the region. It will do so by providing effective support to its portfolio companies, being a business partner of choice, attracting, developing and retaining the best talent, pursuing continuous improvement, and contributing to the social fabric of the United Arab Emirates.

CHAIRMAN’S MESSAGE




What we have endeavoured to achieve through the creation of Mazrui International is to preserve and promote the values of the individual companies that make up the Mazrui portfolio, and at the same time realise the untapped potential of these businesses. Our presence in a number of ‘core’ market sectors reflects our desire to focus on areas where we believe we have a high level of competence and have the ability to ‘add value’. Our clear vision and strategy for each sector is trusted by our partners and customers alike, and is underpinned by the strength of our solutions and our robust service offerings.

Our commitment to continuous improvement allows us to weather changing economic and business climates. We enhance our key internal operations to ensure a consistent and positive experience for our customers, partners, suppliers and employees.

As a company we remain committed to corporate and social responsibility. In all of our actions we strive to ensure that economic, environmental and social factors are considered. We believe that this approach, driven by our values of excellence, integrity and accountability, makes us more responsible and informed as a company.

After more than three decades of success and milestone achievements, we remain committed to be leaders in all that we do, to continually innovate, and to support the social and economic fabric of Abu Dhabi. As always, I am grateful to the Rulers and Government of Abu Dhabi for their confidence and support of Mazrui International and look to the future with excitement and optimism.

Abdullah M. Mazrui
